What Is a Sclerotherapy Injection?

A sclerotherapy injection is a non-surgical treatment designed to eliminate spider veins and small varicose veins. During the procedure, a specially formulated medical solution is injected directly into the unwanted vein. This solution gently irritates the vein lining, causing the vein walls to collapse and seal shut. Once the treated vein closes, your body naturally redirects blood through healthier veins. Over time, the treated vein is gradually absorbed by the body and fades from view. This process creates smoother, clearer-looking skin while improving circulation in the treated area. Because the treatment requires only tiny injections, there are no surgical incisions, stitches, or lengthy recovery periods. Patients often return to normal daily activities shortly after their appointment.
